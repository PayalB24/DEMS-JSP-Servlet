Digital Evidence Management System (DEMS)

The Digital Evidence Management System (DEMS) is designed to meet the growing demands of modern crime investigations by efficiently managing and securing digital evidence. As electronic gadgets and devices continue to evolve, so does the complexity of criminal activities associated with them. Investigators are now faced with large volumes of digital evidence, including hard drives, mobile phones, audio and video files, and more.

Our system aims to simplify this process by providing a secure, easy-to-use platform for managing the entire life cycle of digital evidence. Whether you're part of a law enforcement agency, a forensic analysis lab, or an enterprise dealing with sensitive data, DEMS ensures the integrity and security of your digital evidence every step of the way.

Key Features of the DEMS:

  • Dynamic Field Management: Add custom fields to capture all relevant details for cases and evidence to suit your needs.
  • Secure Upload & Storage: Upload and store digital evidence such as hard disk images, audio files, video files, Call Data Records (CDR), and more with secure access protocols.
  • Efficient Search & Filtering: Quickly search and filter through cases and evidence using powerful tools to speed up investigations.
  • Automated Notifications: Receive email or SMS updates for any changes or updates to cases and evidence, ensuring you're always informed.
  • Comprehensive Reporting: Upload analysis reports and generate detailed reports for case evaluations.
  • Access Control: Multiple levels of user authorization to ensure data is only accessed by authorized personnel.
  • Interactive Dashboard: An intuitive dashboard provides quick insights and visualizations to help you manage evidence more efficiently.
  • Life Cycle Management: From case initiation to final closure, track every stage of evidence and case life cycles to maintain proper documentation.
  • Rule-Based SLA: Set up and manage Service Level Agreements (SLAs) to define the timelines for case handling and evidence analysis.

With DEMS, you can effectively streamline the process of managing and securing digital evidence, ensuring that every piece of evidence is handled with the highest level of care and integrity. Start using our system today to enhance the security and efficiency of your evidence management!
